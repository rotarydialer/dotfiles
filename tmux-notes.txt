TMUX: Terminal Multiplexer

Basics:

	cmd prefix: Ctl-B (by default)

	Screen splitting
		C-b % = horiz
		C-b " = vert

	Pane switching
		C-b arrow
	Pane resizing
		C-b Ctl+arrow

	Naming windows (cover more in scripts section)
		C-b ,


Scripts:
	alf-green-tmux.sh
	alf-dev-tmux.sh

		Scroll: 	C-b [


Sessions:
	tmux list-sessions
	tmux attach -t session_name
	Detach:	C-b d
